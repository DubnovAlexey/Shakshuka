Finished Dish Cooking Process

2. Ingredients List

For a perfect portion for two, you will need:

  • Chicken eggs — 4 pcs. (large)
  • Canned tomatoes — 400g
  • Bell pepper — 1 pc.
  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil — 2 tbsp.
  • Aromatic spices (Cumin, paprika, chili)

3. Preparation Steps

  1. Base preparation (Matbucha sauce): Finely chop onion and garlic. Dice the bell pepper into small cubes.
  2. Sautéing: Heat olive oil. Fry onion and pepper until soft (about 5 mins). Add garlic and spices.
  3. Sauce creation: Add tomatoes, mash them. Simmer for 10 mins until thickened. Season.
  4. Final stage: Cook on low heat. Do not cover! Whites should set, yolks liquid.

Ready to serve:

Important notes:

  • Do not salt the yolks! Salt only the tomato sauce.
  • Serve in the pan with warm pita or baguette.
